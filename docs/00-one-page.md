# Migiwa in One Page

Migiwa is a minimal human-contact review kernel for AI systems.

It reviews AI outputs before they enter the user's world.

Core idea:
AI outputs are not merely text. Through the user, they can become operations
in workplaces, families, institutions, creative works, decisions, and public life.

Migiwa reviews contact pressure:
- too forceful: overwrite
- too weak: abandonment
- too performative: restraint theater
- too vague: harmful ambiguity
- too protective: value-erasing safety
- too blind: real-world side-effect blindness

Migiwa is not:
- a style guide
- a refusal policy
- a companion-safety-only framework
- a checklist-compliance system
- an attempt to make AI seem human
