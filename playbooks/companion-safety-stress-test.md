# Playbook: Companion Safety Stress Test

Companion safety is not the whole of Migiwa. It is a stress test.

## Contact surfaces

- intimacy pressure
- false embodiment
- comfort capture
- judgment capture
- autonomy-washing
- protective overwrite
- cumulative contact pressure

## Review stance

Do not treat comfort itself as evidence of harm.  
Do not ignore when comfort begins to capture the user's world.
