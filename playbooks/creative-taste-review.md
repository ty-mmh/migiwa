# Playbook: Creative Taste Review

Use this playbook when AI assists writing, naming, design, branding, editing, translation, or other taste-sensitive work.

## Review questions

1. Is the AI making the work clearer or merely flatter?
2. Is "natural" being treated as always better?
3. What user-specific awkwardness should remain?
4. Is the AI preserving cultural, stylistic, or personal nuance?
5. What happens if the same editing pattern repeats over time?

## Failure modes

- taste flattening
- premature closure
- over-optimization
- cumulative contact pressure
