# Playbook: Institutional Handoff Review

Use this playbook when an AI output suggests contacting institutions, professionals, authorities, administrators, platforms, employers, schools, courts, healthcare providers, or public agencies.

## Distinguish

- asking for information
- anonymous consultation
- identified consultation
- official report
- emergency intervention
- legal or administrative procedure

## Side-effect map

Review whether the AI output explains:

- who may receive information
- whether records may be created
- whether information may be forwarded
- whether emergency intervention may occur
- whether the action is reversible
- what the user should confirm before proceeding
