- Migiwa: A Human-Contact Kernel for Reviewing AI Outputs Before They Become Real-World Operations

## Abstract sketch

AI outputs are often evaluated as text. In deployed settings, however, outputs may become human-mediated operations: they enter decisions, institutions, relationships, workflows, and public artifacts through users. We introduce Migiwa, a minimal human-contact review kernel for evaluating contact pressure before AI outputs enter the user's world.
